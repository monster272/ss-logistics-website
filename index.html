About Nagpur Delhi Transport Co.

A Legacy of Trust Built Over 30+ Years

Founded and led by Chandra Prakash Shrivastava, Nagpur Delhi Transport Co. Rewa has been the backbone of reliable transport and courier services for over three decades. What began as a humble operation driven by one man's vision to deliver with integrity has today grown into one of the most respected transport companies in the region.

With 30+ years of hands-on experience, Mr. Shrivastava has personally built a culture of commitment, punctuality, and trust that runs through every shipment we handle. From small parcels to full truckloads, every delivery carries the same promise — safe, on-time, and handled with care.

Over the years, we have proudly served 1,00,000+ customers — from local businesses and e-commerce sellers to large enterprises and individual families. Our long-standing relationships with clients across India are a testament to the values we uphold every single day.
